### FAQ Section (NEW - Missing)
**Title:**
> Frequently Asked Questions

**Questions:**

**Q: Do we need special hardware or robotics kits?**
A: No. ElimCrown works on existing laptops, tablets, or computer labs. The virtual robotics lab simulates robots digitally, eliminating the need for expensive physical kits.

**Q: How long does it take to set up?**
A: Most schools are up and running within 1 day. We provide onboarding support and teacher training to ensure smooth adoption.

**Q: Is ElimCrown aligned with CBC?**
A: Yes. ElimCrown is specifically designed for Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum, covering all core competencies and key learning areas.

**Q: How does the evidence capture work?**
A: As students interact with the Playroom, the system automatically captures screenshots, code samples, and activity logs. Teachers review and validate this evidence before it's included in reports.

**Q: Can parents access their child's progress?**
A: Yes. Parents get access to a dedicated portal where they can view their child's activities, evidence portfolios, and competency growth over time.

**Q: What happens after the free trial?**
A: After 30 days, you can choose to continue with a paid plan or cancel. No credit card is required for the trial, and there's no obligation to continue.

**Q: Do you offer training for teachers?**
A: Yes. All plans include initial training. Professional and Enterprise plans include ongoing training and support.

**Q: Can we customize activities for our school?**
A: Enterprise plans include custom activity creation and curriculum alignment specific to your school's needs.
